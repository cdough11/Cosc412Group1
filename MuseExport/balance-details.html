Balance Exercise: Leg Swings

 

How to perform proper leg swings:

  • Start by standing on your right leg and pick your left leg 3 to 6 inches off the floor
  • Keeping your arms at your sides, swing your leg front to back
  • Only touch the floor if you need to regain your balance
  • Repeat this motion for 15 seconds
  • Then, begin swing your leg to the left while holding out your right arm
  • Continue this motion for 15 seconds

 

  • 10 reps total

 

  • 150 points upon completion
